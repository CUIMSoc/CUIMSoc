About Us

Founded in 2018, the Cambridge University Investment Management Society is the largest undergraduate-run fund in the UK. The fund's aim is to promote a general understanding of financial markets by gaining an appreciation of the opportunities and problems involved in managing an investment portfolio. All investment decisions are made by a team of analysts- undergraduate students at the University of Cambridge.
